# Getting Started with Badget

Welcome to Badget! This guide will help you get up and running with our link management platform in just a few minutes.

## What is Badget?

Badget is a powerful link management platform that helps you create, track, and optimize your links. Whether you're a marketer, developer, or content creator, Badget provides the tools you need to manage your links effectively.

## Creating Your Account

1. **Sign Up**: Visit [badget.app](https://badget.app) and click "Sign Up"
2. **Verify Email**: Check your email and click the verification link
3. **Complete Setup**: Add your basic information and preferences

## Creating Your First Link

### Using the Dashboard

1. Click the **"Create Link"** button on your dashboard
2. Enter your destination URL
3. Customize your short link (optional)
4. Add tags and description for organization
5. Click **"Create"** to generate your short link

### Using the API

For developers, you can create links programmatically:

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.badget.app/links \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "url": "https://example.com",
    "alias": "my-first-link"
  }'
```

## Understanding Analytics

Once you've created links, you can track their performance:

- **Click Counts**: See how many times your links have been clicked
- **Geographic Data**: Understand where your audience is located
- **Referrer Sources**: Know where your traffic is coming from
- **Device Information**: Learn about your audience's devices

## Best Practices

### Link Organization

- **Use Tags**: Organize your links with relevant tags
- **Descriptive Aliases**: Create memorable, branded short links
- **Consistent Naming**: Develop a naming convention for your team

### Security

- **Custom Domains**: Use your own domain for better brand trust
- **Access Control**: Manage team permissions appropriately
- **Regular Reviews**: Audit your links periodically

## Quick Tips

1. **Bulk Operations**: Use our bulk import feature for multiple links
2. **QR Codes**: Generate QR codes for offline campaigns
3. **Expiration Dates**: Set expiration dates for time-sensitive campaigns
4. **UTM Parameters**: Add tracking parameters automatically
